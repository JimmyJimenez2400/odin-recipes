Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile, unleavened dough typically made from wheat flour (specifically semolina), water, and sometimes eggs. Formed into various shapes and sizes, it is cooked by boiling or baking and is a staple food deeply rooted in Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • unleavened dough of wheat flour
  • water
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Mix the Dough
  2. Knead the Dough
  3. Let it rest
